☆ LETTER FROM OUR CHAPTER PRESIDENT ☆


Welcome to the official website of the Beta Theta Chapter of Lambda Phi Epsilon at Florida State University.

It is my honor to serve as the president of this chapter for the 2025-2026 school year. Our fraternity was founded on the core principles of authenticity, leadership, and cultural heritage. This chapter prides itself in fostering growth, challenging one another, and creating an environment where young Asian men can learn, thrive, and form lifelong connections.

Having three years of membership in this fraternity, I have learned countless lessons and made lifelong bonds I could have never thought was possible coming into college. I encourage you to explore the website, learn more about our chapters' deep history, and inquire with us if you have any further questions. We hope it provides you with valuable insight into who we are, what we do, and what we stand for.
