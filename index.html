☆ LETTER FROM OUR CHAPTER PRESIDENT ☆

Welcome to the official website of the Florida State University Associate Chapter of Lambda Phi Epsilon International Fraternity, Inc. As the current President for the 2023-2024 academic year, I am profoundly honored to lead a chapter that has become a cornerstone of leadership, philanthropy, and cultural awareness at Florida State University. Throughout my tenure at this esteemed institution, I have witnessed the remarkable evolution of our chapter. We have grown not just in numbers, but in the depth of our brotherhood, continually fostering a nurturing environment for young men of Asian descent to connect, grow, and excel together. Our diversity is our strength, with brothers from various backgrounds, each contributing their unique perspectives and skills, yet all united by our shared commitment to our fraternity's ideals.

As President, my mission is to build upon the legacy of those who came before me, harnessing the individual strengths of each brother to further cement our status as "Leaders Among Men" on campus. Lambda Phi Epsilon has profoundly shaped my life, transforming me into the man I am today and blessing me with a lifelong family. I am eternally grateful for the opportunity to contribute to this eternal brotherhood. I encourage you to explore our website and delve into the rich tapestry of our brotherhood. Thank you for visiting our site. We hope it provides you with a glimpse into the vibrant life and spirit of our chapter.

#49 Carl "Kung FOOd Hustle" Zimmermann
Chapter President
